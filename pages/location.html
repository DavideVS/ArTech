Where can you find us

Our location is in London, UK. That’s why we decided to write in our Blog in English, to get even closer to our daily users. Have started our activity of publishing works of art related to technology for a short time, but we always try to bring new and fresh pieces. The choice of the place fell in this wonderful city full of history and modernism at the level of art, but also of art critics. London is for us a springboard that allows us to get to more people passionate about this world and have continuous exchanges of ideas. The place in opening a project is fundamental and for us this represents the vanguard and the possibility. Thanks to this continuous exchange of information in this area we can make myself known and give a name to our business, which slowly can reach more people and notoriety. In some ways our Blog can also be used to discover new faces of art that until before were hidden or did not have the opportunity to show off.
